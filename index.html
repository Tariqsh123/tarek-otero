About the Author

Tarek Otero is a survivor, storyteller, and youth empowerment coach. Through his memoir, he shares the raw truth of growing up amid chaos and the determined choice to rise above.

With a voice that is both vulnerable and fierce, Tarek has dedicated his life to helping young people find their inner strength. He speaks internationally about resilience, trauma recovery, and the power of never giving up.

“I realized that my past didn't have to be my future. I chose to become the boy who never gave up.”

From the Memoir

Chapter 1 · The Boy who never gave up

“I learned early that silence was a shield. But shields also hide the wounds. Looking back, I realise that the absence of care was louder than any scream. We ran barefoot, hungry for attention, but no one was watching.”

“Dad's old bread truck became our escape, our prison, our everything. One afternoon, a match was struck – not out of malice, but out of a child's longing to feel something. The fire that almost consumed us taught me a lesson: sometimes you have to burn to be reborn.”

— raw, resilient, real

The Healing Journey

1. Break the Silence

Acknowledge the pain — the first step toward freedom.

2. Find Your People

Connect with advocates and communities who listen.

3. Reclaim Your Voice

Your story becomes your power when you share it.

4. Stronger Wings

Rise — not despite the scars, but because of them.

“There was a moment when giving up felt like the only logical choice. The weight of every failure, every lost promise, pressed down until I couldn't breathe. I didn't want to die — I just wanted the pain to stop. But then I remembered the boy who used to climb trees to watch the sunrise. That boy still existed. Hope is not a feeling — it's a decision.”
— Tarek Otero, The Boy Who Never Gave Up
