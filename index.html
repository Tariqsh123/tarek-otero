Tarek Otero — Story Weaver & Illustrator

Born in Seville, raised between libraries and oceans, Tarek has dedicated his life to creating books that feel like lullabies. His award-winning work weaves lyrical prose with watercolor worlds, earning him the Moonbeam Children's Book Award and nominations for the Kate Greenaway Medal.

“Every child holds a universe inside — illustration is the key.”

Before becoming a full-time author, Tarek taught art to young dreamers for a decade, believing that every story starts with a question: “What if?” His books have been translated into 14 languages, inspiring families worldwide.

The Memoir — From Dreams to Pages

Long before the first published copy, Tarek Otero wandered through coastal villages, collecting whispers of wonder. The memoir of "Whispering Lantern" began one autumn night when his daughter asked why the moon followed her. That question became the seed for luminous illustrations and heartfelt prose. Every spread painted in this book reveals memories of Tarek’s own childhood—lost lighthouses, paper boats, and the scent of rain. Through elegant simplicity, he reminds us that children’s books are the first gallery of dreams.

“A lantern can only glow when you dare to open it — just like a story.”

“Tarek’s delicate touch turns each illustration into an emotional compass. A modern classic.” — Kirkus Reviews ★
